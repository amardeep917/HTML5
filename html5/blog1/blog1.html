Top Attractions in Jharkhand

1.Baba Baidyanath Temple

The temple is among the 12 sacred jyotirlingas in the country and is dedicated to Lord Shiva. It also houses idols of Lord Ganesha and Goddess Parvati.

2.Patratu valley

Patratu Valley, located in Jharkhand, is a picturesque valley known for its stunning natural beauty and serene surroundings. Nestled amidst the hills of the Chotanagpur Plateau, this valley offers a captivating escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.

3.Dassam Falls

The Dassam Falls is situated at 34 km from the city of Ranchi on Ranchi-Tata road near the Taimara village. The place is also known as Dassam Garh. The major water source of this waterfall is River Kachni, which falls here from an altitude of 144 feet.
